# Rollback Checklist

## Preconditions
- Verify rollback artifact and migration compatibility are available.
- Confirm incident timeline and ownership before rollback execution.

## Rollback Steps
- Pause new deployments and capture current config snapshots.
- Execute rollback in controlled order and validate service health checks.

## Post-Rollback
- Verify customer-facing error rates and latency recovery.
- Document root cause and guardrail updates.
